About Proguiz

Proguiz is an educational platform built to make learning web development interactive, efficient, and fun. Test your knowledge through flashcards and quizzes designed to help you master HTML, CSS, and JavaScript faster.

Our Mission

We believe learning to code should be engaging and approachable. Our mission is to make complex web topics simple through repetition, instant feedback, and gamified learning experiences.

How It Works

Choose a topic, study interactive flashcards, and then take a quiz to test what you've learned. Your progress is tracked locally, so you can revisit your results anytime and measure improvement.
